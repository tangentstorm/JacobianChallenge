* mathlib enhancements (W6) [/]                                       :mathlib:upstream:

This workstream tracks proposed Mathlib upstream contributions —
universe-polymorphism gaps, missing instances, and infrastructure holes
that block kernel-level goals from sealing.

These are *type-theoretic plumbing items*, not mathematical content.
Lean's universe hierarchy exists to avoid Russell's paradox without
ZFC; classical mathematicians (Jacobi included) worked in a single
Grothendieck universe and never thought about whether `HasCoproducts`
is polymorphic over `w`. The items here are gaps in how Mathlib has
*proven its instances within the universe hierarchy*, not gaps in the
mathematics. Closing them benefits the whole Mathlib ecosystem, not
just this project.

Status of each item: keep "consumer" pointer fresh so when the
upstream lands the project knows where to thread the new annotation.
